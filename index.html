About Me

I'm Kindy Tice, a product of the Midwest although I most recently scratched my California itch for three and a half years. In the Bay Area, I gained valuable work experience in web design and programming and successfully completed a number of courses in both disciplines.

Two events led my career path to becoming a web developer. The first was my natural inclination to help people and be a "man for others." When it came time to choose a job out of college, customer service seemed like a natural fit. My first gig was at iStream Financial Solutions, an electronic payment processing company. This role exposed me to the team of developers, whom I admired personally and professionally. They were the first and last line of defense in the event that the software acts unexpectedly and the unspoken trust non-developers had for them defenitely earned my respect.
